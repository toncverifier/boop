Welcome to Boop + Booper

About Us

We are a small group of diverse individuals who share a love for MapleLegends. We believe in growing together. We make mistakes, and we learn to improve from them. Our guild is mainly composed of members in American, South East Asian, and European time zones.

Our Mission

We strive to be a group capable of completing all bosses. We want to provide our members also a nostalgic social aspect we all grew up with such as GPQ, Events, hangouts, and other bonding activities.

Bossing Services

Boop provides services for bosses such as Scarlion, Targa, and Zakum. Our guild has hosted successful Horntail runs, too. We do all guild runs, or mix it up and bring some of our buddies along.

Join Us!

As of now, Boop has around 40 members and Booper has 60. We plan to maintain this size for a hundred members total. We do recruit, but we would prefer only members who can contribute something to our guilds. This can be in any of the categories of bossing, gpqing, conversations, helping out members, etc. Below are a list of some basic requirements, too :p

Boop

This is our original first guild. The members in here are those who are capable of bossing, want to boss, or wish to eventually do so.

REQUIREMENTS

Level 135+

Attacker jobs: Night Lords, Shadowers, Bowmasters, Marksmen, Dark Knights, Heroes, Paladins, Buccaneers, and Corsairs. Mages: Bishops, Ice Lightning, and Fire Poisons.

HP Washed Attackers

Attackers with enough health to boss Zakum and Horntail are highly suggested/preferred.

Active

As long as there is some consistency, you should be fine. We understand everyone has their own lives, and things can get busy. If you are going to be away for quite some time, just let us know C:

Booper

This is our sister guild we are alliances with that was made only a week after Boop was created. In Booper, you can enjoy the same benefits as those in Boop while still being a more casual player if that is what choose to be.

REQUIREMENTS

Level 70+

Members here range from normally Lv50-100+, new members are now required to be above level 70.

Active

As long as there is some consistency, you should be fine. We understand everyone has their own lives, and things can get busy. If you are going to be away for quite some time, just let us know C:

Interested in our guild

We want players who can contribute to our community such as attending guild pictures, discussions, and events.
